5) Power BI: DAX measures (copy/paste set)
Create these measures in Power BI:

Core

Total Revenue = SUM(fact_bookings[Total_Amount])
Total Bookings = COUNT(fact_bookings[Booking_ID])
Total Rental Days = SUM(fact_bookings[Rental_Days])
Efficiency

Avg Revenue per Booking = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Bookings])
Avg Rental Days = DIVIDE([Total Rental Days], [Total Bookings])
Avg Revenue per Day = AVERAGE(fact_bookings[Amount_per_Day])
Customer behavior

Distinct Customers = DISTINCTCOUNT(fact_bookings[User_ID])
Bookings per Customer = DIVIDE([Total Bookings], [Distinct Customers])
Time intelligence (requires proper Date table marked as Date Table)

Revenue MTD = TOTALMTD([Total Revenue], dim_dates[Booking_Date])
Revenue YTD = TOTALYTD([Total Revenue], dim_dates[Booking_Date])
Revenue MoM % = DIVIDE([Total Revenue] - CALCULATE([Total Revenue], DATEADD(dim_dates[Booking_Date], -1, MONTH)), CALCULATE([Total Revenue], DATEADD(dim_dates[Booking_Date], -1, MONTH)))
